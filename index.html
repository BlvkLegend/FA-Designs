About F.A Designs

A Skill Found Out Of Necessity, Now Passed On

F.A Designs began with a search for a skill that needed no machine, no large workspace, and no major startup capital. Fabric beading was the answer.

Since 2021, the program has trained more than 1,000 students, both online and offline, in a craft that turns fabric and beads into wearable, sellable designs. The training is built for anyone starting from zero: practical, structured, and focused on results you can use right away.
